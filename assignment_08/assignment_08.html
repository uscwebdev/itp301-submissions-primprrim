Final Project Proposal

Topic

I will be creating a Los Angeles restaurant guide and review website. This platform will serve as a comprehensive compilation of my personal experiences and honest thoughts dining at various restaurants across Los Angeles during my time here.

Audience

Anyone and everyone can be the audience! But particularly those who seek an honest guide and review of restuarants in Los Angeles.

Design & Style

The design will embody a minimalist and clean aesthetic, characterized by a simple and streamlined color palette.

The color palette will consist of black, white, and grey which aligns with the overall clean and minimalistic design I'm aiming to achieve.

Website Inspirations: Cabbages World, B14, Werkstatt

Scope

I am planning on creating around 6-9 pages (this may change). The pages will include a homepage hosting all the featured restaurants and review pages of each restaurant displayed.

Extras

I'm planning on implementing event-driven DOM Manipulation and a React Library/libraries (this may change).
